## Manual Visual Verification

- Open the app in a WebGPU-capable browser at desktop width and confirm the default canvas has no preset pins until the radar control is clicked.
- Click the radar control and confirm a center-origin pulse expands from the viewport center.
- With saved presets available, confirm discovery pins are limited to a small set and show chevrons above for shallower destinations and below for deeper destinations.
- Confirm chevron animation becomes more pronounced for larger zoom-depth gaps while the number of visible chevrons remains capped.
- Pan or zoom after activation and confirm discovery mode exits.
- Open one or more settings popups and activate the radar; off-screen edge markers should stay inside the visible safe frame rather than under the top bar, bottom controls, or popups.
- On a mobile-width viewport, confirm the radar control remains visible as a compact icon button and edge markers avoid the mobile controls.
